Leo Tolstoy, 1828-1910

Overview

Tolstoy is one of the greatest authors of all time. The verisimilitude of his writing is such that you forget you're even reading a book. "If the world could write itself, it would write like Tolstoy." -Isaac Babel

Best Works, No Order

  • Anna Karenina
  • War and Peace
  • The Death of Ivan Ilyich
